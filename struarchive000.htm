Welcome to Summaries and Previews, the Archive of all Games played here at ST:RU.

Major events or the completion of major missions mark the end of one Game and the beginning of another. For the players, the separation into different "Games" is almost non-existent. However, when the Gamemaster feels as though a portion of the storyline provided enough closure to be a story all by itself, it becomes a "Game".
